Ground Beef Bulgogi

<

Description

Korean BBQ is one of the best cuisines that i have ever had the pleasure of consuming Here is a simple recipe that allos you to create this great dish without all of the fancy ingredients and tools.

Remember that this is from the homegirl at My Heart Beats and is not an original recipe from HR

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 inch knkob ginger minced
  • 1 lb of ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 5 tbsp coconut aminos
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teasppn salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 -3 green onions
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Heat oil in saute pan over medium heat. Add the garlic and ginger and stir fry for a minute
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until mostly browned
  3. Next add the coconut sugar, coconut aminos, sesame oil and spices to the pan.<
    Mix well and continue to cook for another 4-5 minutes
  4. Turn off the head, add the green onions and sesame seeds. Mix then serve
