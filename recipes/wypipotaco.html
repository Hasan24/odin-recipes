<

Description

Sometimes you want the finest, most authentic, mouth watering mexican food. Other nights you just want to throw something together that the family will eat and you don't need that extra "culture"

This is no offense to the homegirl Alexandra cooks. I make these tacos too and Im a black man

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • a packet of taco seasoning MILD dont forget it
  • yellow corn tortilla
  • sour cream
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • Old El Paso Salsa
  • can of green chile and tomato

Steps

  1. Add the ground beef and cook until mostly browned. Drain the meat and return to stove
  2. Add in the taco seasoning packet and just enough water to cover the poweder you just poured in. Mix well and cook at low heat
  3. Add in the can of tomatoes and chili. Drain water out first
  4. dump mixture into taco shell. Add cheese and sour cream if you like
